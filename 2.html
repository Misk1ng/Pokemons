What Are Pokémon?

Pokémon are creatures with special powers. Trainers catch them using Pokéballs and train them to battle other Pokémon. Each Pokémon belongs to one or two types, like Fire, Water, Electric, etc.

Popular Starter Pokémon

  • Bulbasaur – Grass/Poison
  • Charmander – Fire
  • Squirtle – Water
  • Chikorita – Grass
  • Cyndaquil – Fire
  • Totodile – Water

Trainer Tips

  • Use type advantages to win battles (e.g. Water beats Fire).
  • Level up your Pokémon to evolve them into stronger forms.
  • Carry a balanced team with different types for flexibility.

Fun Pokémon Facts

Did you know Pikachu was originally not going to be the series mascot? Clefairy was considered first!

There are Pokémon based on real animals, objects, and even food.
